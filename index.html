Human Freedom Index

The Human Freedom Index (HFI) is based on a wide range of measures that describe the state of human freedom, including personal, civil and economic freedom. The index represents a broad measure of human freedom and is understood as lacking coercive constraints. It uses 79 different indicators of personal and economic freedom. The index was created in 2015 and covers 159 countries from 2008 to 2017.
